Publications and Projects

Parameter Estimation of Modelica Building Models Using CasADi

Developed from my master's thesis, this project was presented as a paper at the 15th International Modelica Conference in Aachen, Germany, held from October 9-11, 2023.

Accomplishments
  • Tools: Modelica, FMI, CasADi, Gray-box modeling
  • This research focuses on enhancing building energy efficiency through advanced predictive control techniques. By employing gray-box model identification, which merges theoretical models with real-world data, the study addresses the limitations of traditional building modeling approaches. It evaluates innovative parameter estimation methods, demonstrating the superior performance of multiple shooting techniques. This work not only contributes to energy optimization in buildings but also showcases my ability to apply complex modeling and data analysis skills in practical applications.
